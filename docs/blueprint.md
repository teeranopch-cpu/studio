# **App Name**: StudentGov Portal

## Core Features:

- Student Council & Personnel Registry: Manage detailed records of current and past student council committees, administrators, and faculty advisors, including their roles and tenure within the organization.
- Civic Education Hub: Provide a centralized platform to access intensive academic content on the Democratic System, Thai political history, the Constitution, and international children's rights, featuring videos, songs, and training materials.
- Legal Document & Regulation Archive: Act as a secure repository for the Student Council Constitution (various versions), school announcements, and Ministry of Education regulations regarding student conduct and disciplinary measures.
- Activity & Partnership Portfolio: Showcase comprehensive performance reports, activity photos for each academic year, and details of collaborative Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) with external organizations.
- External Resource & Forms Portal: Offer categorized links to external learning resources, academic grading systems, and provide downloadable digital forms for election applications and public relations materials.
- AI Document Simplifier: An AI tool to summarize and simplify complex legal documents and academic texts into easily understandable language for students and parents.
- Unified Search & Filter: Implement a comprehensive search and filtering system across all modules to quickly locate personnel, specific documents, educational content, and activity records.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident, professional blue-green (#2994CC) to convey trust and intellect for official information.
- Background color: A very light, subtle cool white (#EBF4F8) provides a clean, open, and professional canvas, enhancing readability.
- Accent color: A refreshing aqua (#30CEBD) used for interactive elements and key highlights to draw attention without overpowering.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly legible characteristics, suitable for detailed academic and legal content.
- Use clear, professional, and consistent outline icons that visually represent governance, education, documentation, and communication themes to maintain a formal aesthetic.
- Implement a structured, grid-based layout prioritizing clarity and hierarchy, ensuring content is easily digestible and navigation is intuitive across all information categories.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for content loading, page changes, and interactive elements to provide a polished and unobtrusive user experience, maintaining a professional feel.
